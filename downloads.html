Emulators - My personal favorite emulators

All of these files will still need roms, which I can't provide ;)

Best all around emulator

  • RetroArch [OpenSource] - Several cores for a multitude of devices, Runs on most devices; Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS

    • iOS

  • Delta - Retro Nintendo emulator for iOS. Runs NES, SNES N64, GBC, GBA, DS

    • Honestly My experience with Delta has been poor, Not due to the software itself but because of all the restrictions Apple put on their devices.

    You have to setup AltStore on both your pc and iOS device and refresh it every 7 days, and if you forget to do that you will be locked out of it.

    Android

  • Lemuroid [OpenSource] - Retro Emulator for android with vast support for older systems

    • In my experience it was really easy to setup, took like 10 minutes to figure things out and it handles really well. As soon as I get rid of my iPhone I'm Switching to Lemuroid.
